00 — Introduction
About DealerVoice

Context, access details, and how to read this report.

What DealerVoice Is

DealerVoice is a consumer-facing automotive dealership review and trust platform. Buyers discover dealers by country and city, read verified-style reviews, request quotes, and browse inventory. Dealers claim profiles, manage teams, respond to reviews, and upgrade via Razorpay subscriptions.

The product evolved from a B2B dealership voice SaaS concept into a consumer review platform with Day 1 monetization — affiliate ads, AdSense, subscriptions, sponsorships, and lead fees.

Strategic Position

India-first GTM with deep city coverage (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad guides) while the product architecture supports worldwide launch — US, UK, AU geo pages, global ads, and country inventory browse.

All dealer billing settles in INR via Razorpay only (no Stripe). SUPER_ADMIN tracks every revenue stream in the unified Platform income dashboard.

Contact & Outreach

Dealer outreach: dealers@dealervoice.io

Social / press: outreach@dealervoice.io (not shown on public site — department addresses kept separate per policy)

LinkedIn press brief shipped for flagship research article on AI + automotive trust.
